### Component Specifications

**Time Filter:**
- Segmented button
- Options: Week, Month, Year, All Time
- Right side of header

**Summary Cards:**
- 4 cards in row
- Large number (36px)
- Label below (14px)
- Background: Surface Container

**Reading Time Chart:**
- Full width
- Bar chart
- Y-axis: Hours
- X-axis: Time periods based on filter
- Summary line below

**Genre Distribution:**
- Pie or donut chart
- 200px diameter
- Legend beside or below
- Clickable segments

**Streak Widget:**
- Current streak with emoji
- Best streak
- Monthly activity
- Heatmap or calendar view
