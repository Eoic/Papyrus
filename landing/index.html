Everything you need, nothing you don't

Organize your way

  • Shelves and collections for flexible grouping
  • Color-coded tags for instant visual filtering
  • Series tracking with reading order
  • Track physical books alongside digital ones
  • Advanced search across your entire library

Read comfortably anywhere

  • Integrated reader for EPUB, PDF, MOBI, AZW3, TXT, CBR, CBZ
  • Customizable typography: font, size, spacing, margins
  • Light, dark, sepia, and custom themes
  • Reading profiles for different devices and contexts
  • Optimized for e-ink displays

Capture every thought

  • Multi-color highlights with inline notes
  • Standalone book notes
  • Bookmarks for quick navigation
  • Search across all annotations
  • Export to text, PDF, or Markdown

Track your reading life

  • Automatic reading time and page tracking
  • Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly goals
  • Streaks and achievements
  • Visual charts: trends, genre distribution, completion rates

One app. Every platform.
